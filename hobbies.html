How I'd Teach a Friend to Ride

Learning to snowboard is a sequence, but each step hides a few smaller details. Here's the ordered path with the finer points nested underneath:

  1. Get comfortable strapped in
    • Skate with one foot out to reach the lift
    • Practice standing up on flat ground
  2. Master the heel-side slide
    • Keep weight centered over the board
    • Use your toes to feather your speed
  3. Link your first turns
    • Look where you want to go, not at your feet
    • Lead with the front shoulder
  4. Hit the green runs and have fun
